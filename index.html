Welcome to Uttarakhand

Known as the "Land of Gods", Uttarakhand is a treasure trove of natural beauty, from the snow-capped Himalayas to pristine rivers and lush valleys. Come, explore, and immerse yourself in the adventure and spirituality that Uttarakhand has to offer.

Top Destinations

Nainital

The beautiful lake district of India, known for its peaceful surroundings and scenic beauty.

Rishikesh

A hub for yoga, spirituality, and adventure sports like rafting on the Ganges River.

Mussoorie

The Queen of Hills offers stunning views of the Doon Valley and snow-capped peaks.

Haridwar

Haridwar is an ancient city, known for its religious significance and the Ganga Aarti.
